About Me

For 15 years I’ve been working as an Art Director for a Screen Printer in Huntington Beach. I handle all design work, oversee customer supplied artwork, and maintain the company web sites. It’s easy work and low stress so it’s been an ideal arrangement for my cautious disposition.


One day I realized I was falling behind. Technology has been racing ahead at full speed while I’ve just been floating along thinking I’m keeping up. It was an unpleasant phone call with GoDaddy tech support that made me realize I’m not comfortable being the technologically handicapped person this person made me out to be.


So here I am, bound and determined not be become obsolete. On the contrary, I plan to put myself right on the cutting-edge, with the skills of a full stack developer and an experienced graphic designer. The potential for this experience to prove life changing is pretty good and, I admit, I’m approaching it with some trepidation. But as long as I have my coffee first, I’m good.
